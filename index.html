Background
& Audience
Tourism & Events Queesland wanted to increase consideration of The Whitsundays amongst 'soicla fun seekers'; digitally savvy consumers that socialise substantially online.
Problem
The Whitsundays was losing its cachet as an aspirational holiday location
Objective
Shift perceptions and re-build the Whitsundays' social currency as a world-class destianation.
Solution
We knew movies rated high on the list of our target’s interests and, with incredible beaches, seaolanes, yatchs and glamorous locations, the Whitsudays is the perfect setting for a movie
So we invited Australians to write it:
The Whitsudays.
A movie that's waiting to be written
We enlisted world-renowned screenwriter Craig Pearce as Script Supervisor. Then for 20 days we posted a daily storyboard image on Facebook and Instagram, inviting the public to write scenes for a change to win a $10,000 luxury holiday.
